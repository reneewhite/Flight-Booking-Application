ABOUT US

Welcome to SA Flights South African Airways (SOC) Ltd (SAAT) is a full-service MRO organisation, the largest in Africa.
SAAT has held full and uninterrupted US Federal Aviation Authority certification since the late nineteen eighties, serving a
range of local and international airlines.
Prior to 1 April 2000, SAAT was a division of SAA. SAA Technical was corporatized on 1 April 2000 and fully registered
as SAA Technical (SOC) Limited to become a wholly owned subsidiary of SAA (SOC) Ltd.

SAA is sole provider of aircraft maintenance to SAA, Mango and Comair/Kulula which are the premier carriers in South Africa.
SAA Technical also provides MRO services to a variety of regional and other airlines such as Air Namibia, Rwandair, TAAG, Arik Air,
Trans Air Congo, Korongo Airlines,
Air Zimbabwe, Air Mauritius, Air France, KLM and Turkish Airlines among others.
Market segments include: major airframe checks; engine overhaul; mechanical components; avionics; and line maintenance.
SAAT’s main operational base is Johannesburg and it also services customers in Cape Town, Durban and other regional airports.
